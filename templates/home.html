What is Heart Disease?

Issues affecting the heart’s structure or function.

Common Causes

Hypertension, high cholesterol, smoking, obesity, diabetes.

Symptoms

Chest pain, breathlessness, fatigue, palpitations, dizziness.

Risk Factors

Age, genetics, poor diet, inactivity, stress.

Prevention

Healthy diet, regular exercise, no smoking, manage stress.

Treatment

Medication, procedures, lifestyle changes.
